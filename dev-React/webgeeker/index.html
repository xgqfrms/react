Our platform

webgeeker makes it easy for businesses to use whatsapp as a medium of communication with their customers! Be it notifying their customers about something, expanding USSD and sms service to whatsapp, orders tracking, run a campaign or a survey, provide a subscription channel for a particular service and many more.

We have developed the platform from ground up with great emphasis on perfomance and high availabiliby. The platform scales perfectly and we have several system monitors in place to make sure that your service is automatically restored and restarted if anything happens.
